POST_ID: TEST_FINALIZER_SUCCESS-P02

OVERLAY_HOOK_1: The prayers that bounced were not ignored, they were declined because God is not your vending machine.
OVERLAY_HOOK_2: Stop asking God to take away your pain and start asking Him what He wants to do with it.
OVERLAY_HOOK_3: God declines comfort prayers because He is building something in you that comfort would destroy.
OVERLAY_CTA: read caption

CAPTION:
You have been praying for years. You ask God to take the pain. You ask Him to remove the obsession, fix the relationship, clear the fog. And the ceiling stays quiet.

You concluded God is not listening.

That is the wrong conclusion. God is not ignoring your prayers. He is declining them, because that is not what He does. He is not a janitor for your self-inflicted problems. He is not a vending machine you insert suffering into and receive relief from. He is a King, and He is looking for men who want to know Him, not just extract from Him.

The prayers that bounced were selfish prayers. Help me feel better. Take my pain away. Fix what I broke. Every one of those prayers had you at the center and God as the solution to your comfort problem. That is not a relationship with a Father. That is a transaction with a vending machine that stopped working.

Here is what changes when you approach it correctly: Are you a petitioner or a partner on God's mission?

When you come to God asking what He wants from you instead of what you want from Him, the dynamic shifts. Prayer becomes conversation. Scripture becomes revelation. The pain you were begging to escape becomes the exact thing He uses to make you useful.

God is deeply interested in your suffering. He just is not interested in ending it before it forms something in you worth building with.

Today: Pray once without mentioning your problem. Ask only: what do You want me to do for You today?
