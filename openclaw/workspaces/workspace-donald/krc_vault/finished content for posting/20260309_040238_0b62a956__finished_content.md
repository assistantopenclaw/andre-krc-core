The logic of your current position cannot produce the breakthrough you are hoping for. That version of you built the stuck place. A different version is required to leave it.

Faith is the bridge between where you are and where God is pointing. You do not cross a bridge by waiting for it to come to you.

Comfort Is the Reason You Are Still Here

Most men think comfort is neutral. A recovery zone. The thing you earn by working hard and the thing you return to in between seasons of effort.

It is not neutral. For a man with something to build, comfort is the mechanism of stagnation.

Here is how the loop works. Something in your life is not where it should be. The relationship, the finances, the calling you have not stepped into, the talent you have not developed. The gap between where you are and where you know you are supposed to be creates discomfort. So you soothe it. You sit down. You put something on in the background. You numb the signal that was trying to move you. And tomorrow the gap is still there, but the urgency has been sedated.

Repeat that pattern for a year and nothing has moved. Not because you are lazy. Because comfort is effective at killing urgency, and urgency is the only thing that makes movement possible.

You do not rise when you are comfortable. That is not an opinion. It is the design.

The calling costs something. The thing you are supposed to build requires the same energy you are currently spending on not feeling the gap. You cannot use comfort to recover from the discomfort of not moving. You can only use movement.
