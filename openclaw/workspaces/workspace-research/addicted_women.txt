A recruitment error requires a standards correction. The drive toward a partner is not the problem. God built that drive. What you have been doing is hiring liabilities for that role and calling yourself sick when the hire keeps costing you everything.

If the list is empty, your recruitment was the problem, not your character.

A woman who loves Christ, who is stable in faith, who multiplies your capacity for the mission, that woman is attractive in a category that lasts. You have just never trained your eyes to see that category because you have been focused on a different one entirely.


Today: List your ex-partner's top three spiritual habits. If the list is empty, say out loud: my recruitment criteria was the problem, and I am changing it now.
